## About

A fully interactive security awareness website that educates users about phishing and social engineering threats through comprehensive educational content, real-world examples, and an engaging quiz.

---

## Features

- 🌐 **Bilingual** — Full Arabic and English support with one-click switching
- 🎯 **Interactive Quiz** — 10 real-world Saudi-context phishing scenarios
- 🔀 **Randomized** — Different questions and answer order every session
- 🔍 **Real Brand Examples** — Side-by-side fake vs. real comparisons for Absher, Nafaz, Amazon, and STC Pay
- 📊 **Live Participant Counter** — Shared real count across all visitors
- 📋 **Instant Answer Review** — See what you got wrong and why
- 💡 **Personalized Tips** — Security advice tailored to your score
- 📤 **Share Results** — One-tap sharing via WhatsApp and X (Twitter)
- 📱 **Fully Responsive** — Works on all screen sizes
- ⚡ **Zero Dependencies** — Single HTML file, no server or setup required

---

## What You'll Learn

- **What is Phishing?** — SMS (Smishing), Email Spoofing, Voice Calls (Vishing), Social Media scams
- **How Attacks Work** — The four-step playbook behind every phishing attempt
- **Real vs. Fake** — Visual comparison of legitimate and fraudulent versions of Absher, Nafaz, Amazon, and STC Pay
- **How to Protect Yourself** — 6 practical steps including MFA, link inspection, OTP safety, and reporting
